Global Fastener Forum 2026 年全球扣件產業論壇

2026 Global Fastener Forum

Driving the Future : The Next Decade of Taiwan’s Fastener Industry

As global supply chains are reshaped and smart manufacturing accelerates, the manufacturing sector is entering a new phase of transformation. From automation and digitalized management to global supply chain strategies, competitiveness is no longer defined by production capability alone but by the ability to integrate technologies and foster cross-industry collaboration.

As a key player in the global market, Taiwan’s fastener industry is built on a strong foundation of precision and reliability. This platform brings together leading experts and industry pioneers to explore emerging trends, exchange insights, and identify new opportunities shaping the future of the fastener industry.

Highlights

Policy

Redefining Industry
Entry Barriers

Explore the structural shifts driven by CBAM, tariffs, and global trade policies. Policy is no longer a backdrop but a decisive force shaping order acquisition, pricing strategies, and global positioning.

Market

From Product Supply to Strategic Partnership

Buyer criteria worldwide are shifting rapidly. Beyond price, factors such as carbon emissions, regulatory compliance, lead times, and supply stability are becoming the keys to securing long-term partnerships.

Industry

From Manufacturing to Value Creation

Smart manufacturing, digitalization, and ESG are reshaping competitive models, enabling the fastener industry to move toward higher-value production and integrated solutions.
